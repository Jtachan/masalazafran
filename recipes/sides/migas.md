# Migas

One very traditional Spanish dish is "migas", cooked out of stale bread.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Stale bread_
- _Garlic_ (3~6 cloves)
- _Chorizo_
- _Ham in cubes_ (optional)
- _Green peas_ (optional)
- _Egg_ (optional)
- _Paprika powder_
- _Olive oil_

---

**Steps**

1. Break the bread in small pieces that can easily be eaten.
2. Put a decent amount of oil in a pan, then stir-fry in it the garlic (chopped in thik slices), the chorizo and the ham. Cook for :clock: 5~7 minutes.
3. Lower the heat to medium-low, then add the bread, the green peas and the paprika powder. Let it all cook for :clock: 10 minutes.
4. You can top the plate with a fried egg on top.

!!! Tip
    This dish is a great combination together with other "tapas", like "Pimientos de padron" or "Patatas bravas".
