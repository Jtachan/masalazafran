# Kiwi-Jam

A sweet and sour jam, perfect for toasts with butter.

!!! Tip
    This recipe can use any other fruit/berry.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Kiwis_ (800 g)
- _Sugar_ (100 g)
- _Water_ (200 mL)

---

**Steps**

1. Peel the kiwis and chop them in small pieces (eights).
2. Put the kiwis in a pot with the water and half the sugar. Put the lid to the pot and cook it for :clock: 15 minutes.
3. Blend everything together. You can leave it with some small pieces or leave everything as a pure.
4. Cook everything again at medium heat with the rest of the sugar. Keep stirring for :clock: 5 minutes.
5. Let it sit at room temperature before pouring it into containers.
