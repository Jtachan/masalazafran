# Gnocchi

Gnocchi are a great type of pasta made out of potato or sweet potato.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Potato_ or _Sweetpotato_ (550 g)
- _Flour_ (200 g)
- _Salt_

---

**Steps**

1. Boil the potatoes until they are soft. The original recipe calls to not peel them beforehand for a better starch control, but you do you.
2. Mash them and mix them with the flour until a homogeneous dough is formed. Let it rest for :clock: 10~15 minutes.
3. Divide the dough into smaller parts, then stretch each part into a fat string (as fat as a finger).
4. Chop the string into "small pillows". You can leave them like this or shape them with a fork.
