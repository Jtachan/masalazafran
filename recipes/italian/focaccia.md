# Focaccia

The focaccia is an Italian bread that is prepared with a slow fermentation.
It is very easy to prepare and requires no kneading for the dough.
Once prepared, it is perfect for eating as a sandwich or as a dip with sauces.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Bread flour_ (450 g)
- _Water_ (350 mL)
- _Yeast_ (5 g)
- _Salt_ (9 g)
- _Olive oil_
- _Desired topping_\*

!!! Note
    The topping to put on top of the focaccia can be almost anything, but vegetables are recommended.

---

**Steps**

1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl until they are fully integrated.
2. Let the bowl rest in the fridge for :clock: 12~24 hours.
3. Spread the dough over an oiled trey and let it rest :clock: 2~4 hours.
4. Put oil on your hands, then introduce the fingers (while being separated) on the dough to create extra bubbles on the surface. Do not knead!
5. Preheat the oven at 220 °C. Meanwhile, put the toppings and olive oil on top of the focaccia.
6. Bake in the oven for :clock: 25-30 minutes.
