# Fresh Pasta

Pasta is a very simple recipe that can be shaped into many different forms: spaghetti, lasagna sheets, ravioli, etc.
Any fresh pasta can be easily stored in the freezer and only needs 2~4 minutes to cook.

!!! Hint
    See also the recipe for [Green Pasta](green_pasta.md)

---

**Ingredients** (per ratio)

- _Egg_ (1 M)
- _Flour_ (100 g)

!!! Hint
    The egg can be replaced with 50 mL water to make vegan pasta.

---

**Steps**

1. Mix all ingredients into a homogeneous dough. Do not use salt!
2. Knead the dough for :clock: 10 minutes until it becomes elastic.
3. Cover the dough with some clear wrap and let it rest for :clock: 30 minutes.
4. Shape into the desired form.
