# Easy Pizza Dough

A quick an easy recipe for a pizza dough without the need to wait overnight.
This recipe is for one pizza of approx 28 cm diameter.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Pizza flour_ (260 g)
- _Yeast_ (12 g fresh / 4 g dry)
- _Look-warm water_ (170 mL)
- _Olive oil_ (10 g)
- _Salt_ (4 g)
- _Sugar_ (4 g)

---

**Steps**

1. Disolve the **sugar** and **yeast** in the **water**. Let it rest :clock: 5~10 minutes to _activate it_<sup>1</sup>.
2. In a clear bowl set the **flour** and make a whole in the center. Add in it the **salt and oil** and integrate everything.
3. Integrate the **water** with the mix until it becomes a dough. Knead everything for :clock: 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
4. Let it sit for :clock: 1~2 hours until it doubles the size.
5. Set the dough on a floured surface, then make a ball generating tension on the surface. Let it rest for another :clock: 40~60 minutes.
6. Spread the dough into a pizza shape by first stretching it on the sides, then with the tip of the fingers pressing from the center to the sided.
7. Prepare the pizza as desired, then bake at 250 °C for :clock: 30~50 minutes.

!!! Notes
    1. No matter the yeast, it should be activated for better results. An active yeast will generate small bubbles on the surface of the water.
