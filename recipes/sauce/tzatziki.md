# Tzatziki

The tzatziki is a refreshing mediterranean sauce, which is great to accompany by meat or bread.

---

**Ingredients**

- _Cucumber_ (1)
- _Garlic_ (2~3 cloves)
- _Greek yoghurt_
- _Lemon juice_ (1/2 lemon)
- _Mint_ or _dill_
- _Olive oil_ (10 g)
- _Salt_

---

**Steps**

1. Grate the cucumber on a strainer to separate its flesh from its juice.
2. Chop very fine the garlic and the herbs.
3. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and adjust the salt to taste.
