# Quiche

The Lorraine (french) quiche is a great dish to take to a potluck to share with others.
Easy to cook and tasty.

<img alt="Lorraine Quiche" src="../../_imgs/quiche.jpeg" width=500>

---

**Ingredients**

- _Quiche dough_
- _Eggs_ (4 medium)
- _Milk_ (250 mL)
- _Shredded cheese_

For the filling:

- _Cubed bacon_
- _Spinach_
- _Red bell pepper_
- _Spring onion_

!!! Notes
    The filling can be done with any other ingredients.

---

**Steps**

1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C. Lay the quiche dough on a circular container and stab its base with a fork all over the dough. Cook the dough for :clock: 10 minutes.
2. Precook all the filling (separately) in a pan or with the desired method. Reserve each ingredient without any additional fat.
3. Whisk the eggs with salt and pepper, then mix it with the milk.
4. Pour over the dough the eggs mix with the filling ingredients and the cheese.
5. Cook for :clock: 30~35 minutes. You can optionally lower the temperature to 150 °C. Let it rest :clock: 5 minutes before serving.

!!! Hint
    After baking, garnish the quiche with chives and/or Parmesan.
